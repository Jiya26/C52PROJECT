Camel

Features Of Camel

  1. At least one hump on their backs.
  2. Long curved necks.
  3. Broad, large feet.
  4. Tufted tails.

Chameleon

Features Of Chameleon

  • These species come in a range of colors, and many species have the ability to change color.
  • Chameleons are distinguished by their zygodactylous feet.
  • their very extensive, not highly modified, rapidly extrudable tongues.
  • their swaying gait; and crests or horns on their brow and snout.

Cicada

Features Of Cicada

  • Cicadas are medium to large in size, ranging from 2 to 5 cm.
  • Cicada, any of a group of sound-producing insects that have two pairs of membranous wings, prominent compound eyes, and three simple eyes.
  • Male cicadas produce loud noises by vibrating membranes near the base of the abdomen.
  • Eggs are usually laid in woody plant tissues that drop from the plant when, or shortly after, the eggs hatch.
